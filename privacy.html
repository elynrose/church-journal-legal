Privacy Policy

Church Journal (“the App”) is a personal journal for worship songs, sermon notes, and scriptures. This policy explains how we handle information when you use the App.

Summary

  • Your journal entries are stored only on your device.
  • We do not require an account.
  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not use analytics or advertising SDKs in the App.

Information stored on your device

The App stores the following locally on your phone or tablet:

  • Journal entries (worship songs, notes, and scripture references)
  • Dates and service times (AM/PM) associated with entries
  • Your custom song additions or edits, if any

This data stays on your device unless you back up your device through your operating system’s backup service (for example, Google Backup or iCloud). We do not receive or access that backup data.

Information we do not collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store on our servers:

  • Your name, email, or contact details
  • Your journal content
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics

Notifications

The App can send optional local reminders (morning and evening) to encourage journaling and scripture reading. Notification permission is requested by your device’s operating system. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings. Reminder scheduling uses your device’s local timezone; that information is not sent to us.

Bundled content

The App includes an offline Bible and a worship song library bundled in the application. Reading or searching that content does not send information to us.

Third-party services

The App does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or social login services. App store providers (Google Play, Apple App Store) may collect information according to their own policies when you download or update the App.

Children’s privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data retention and deletion

Because data is stored on your device, you can delete it by clearing the App’s data in your device settings or uninstalling the App.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page will change when we do. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.
